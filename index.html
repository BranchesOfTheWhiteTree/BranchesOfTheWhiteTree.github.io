Welcome to our Website!

Greetings Traveller, Trailblazer, and/or Captain! Welcome to the Branches of the White Tree! This is a non-profit fan-made website for the Hoyoverse games: Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, and Honkai Impact 3rd.

In our website, you'll find many lore-related write-ups and compendiums pertaining to the games. Additionally, we are trying our best to add guides and some basic theorycrafting information about the characters.

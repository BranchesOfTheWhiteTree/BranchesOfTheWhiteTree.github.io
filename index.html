Welcome to our Website!

Greetings Traveller, Trailblazer, and/or Captain! Welcome to the Branches of the White Tree! This is a non-profit fan-made website for the Hoyoverse games: Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, and Honkai Impact 3rd.

In our website, you'll find many lore-related write-ups and compendiums pertaining to the games. Additionally, we are trying our best to add guides and some basic theorycrafting information about the characters.

While we mostly concentrate on Genshin Impact and Honkai: Star Rail, we will add information regarding Honkai Impact 3rd when time permits. However, being a very big game with quite a lot to catch-up, we won't promise the content to be up-to-date for Honkai Impact 3rd. For the remaining two games, we will try our best to make it as up-to-date as possible.

This website is a culmination of many people's hard work and interest put to make a wonderful archive of information for all players of the franchise. We, the members of this website were originally total strangers who met each other digitally via many twitch streamers sharing the same interest and passion.

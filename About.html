About the team

About me

Greetings Travellers! My name is Arun. I'm a life-long student, 73% dreamer and a 27% realist. In my day-job, I work as a content writer and subject expert in an EdTech company. Outside of work, I'm a nerdy introvert who has interests in science, maths, coding, solving puzzles, teaching, reading, writing, and gaming.

Yeah, that's a lot of hobbies 🤣. However, I don't do all of them at once. Nevertheless, you'll rarely find me idle and bored.

I've been playing Genshin Impact since 31st July 2021, and I love this game a lot. After more than a year of playing the game, and mastering the lore and meta as much as possible, I wanted to share my love for the game with other travellers like you. So, I created this website and made it available for anyone and everyone to get a lot of relevant information about the game.

And ofcourse, as a single person, it will be quite tedious and time-consuming to write each and every aspect of the game. Hence, I approached a trusted few members from BranOnline twitch community, and they have been quite generous and sweet in lending a hand to my passion project.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the team need?

We need people who are enthusiastic and willing to contribute content to the website regarding Genshin Impact.

If you are an active player of the game and meet the below listed mandatory prerequisited, you can contribute to the project in some way, shape, or form. Based on your strengths and interests, we'll figure something out for you.

Are there any prerequisites needed for contributing to the project?

We have a few mandatory requirements.

  • You should be a member of BranOnline Discord Server, as we'll post all the updates there, and we'll have a lot of discussions there.
  • You should be comfortable to communicate in English through either discord text or discord voice call.

What are the ways I can contribute to the project?

At the moment, you can contribute to the project in the following ways:

  • Genshin lore writer - you help in writing the content for characters, quests, stories, world structure, etc.,
  • Genshin guide maker - if you are interested in combat, or do combat theorycrafting, or both.
  • Genshin fact checker - we need people to verify the content details provided by the writer folks.
  • Genshin combat tester - we need people to cross-check the calculations done by the theorycrafting folks.
  • Pure content writer - if you are good with writing in general, then you can help those who have some content, but have difficulty penning it down.
  • Website play testers - we need people to look at the user-interfaces of the interactable pages and give us feedbacks to improve.

This contribution list is rather provisional. We'll keep updating information.

Where should I register and how should I submit my work?

  • We will circulate a google form in the discord channel asking for volunteers/contributors with their contact details and topics of interest.
  • We will wait a few days for the interested folks to register.
  • Once the registration form is closed, we'll take a few days to look at all the submissions and set-up the logistics.
  • And once the form assessment is done, we'll send you the details about the format in which the content should be submitted, and to whom to submit, etc.,
  • Depending on the topic, we'll set-up soft deadlines, and the volunteers can start working on them.
  • In case of any doubts, please message some of the core team members for clarifications.
  • We'll send a reminder as we approach the soft-deadline.
  • After the soft-deadline, we'll forward your work to the fact-checkers or combat testers for cross-checking your work
  • Once we get a green signal from there, members of the core team will have a final look. Then, it will be published in the website!
  • Then the core team will reflect upon what worked well, what did not work, and will compare the published information with the road map we have for the next cycle.

What happens if I miss any deadlines?

We understand that your contribution to this project is purely on a voluntary basis. And that if you are contributing to it, you are actively taking time out of your busy schedule to do so. So, we won't enforce any hard deadlines on you.

The soft deadlines are there so that we can pass your work to the next person in the process. That is, if you are a writer, we'll pass your work to a fact-checker. And if you are a fact-checker, we'll pass your work to the core team for final review and then to the website to publish it.

If suppose you cannot meet the soft deadline, you submit how much ever progress you've made so far. We'll take the work you've done for that cycle. And you can submit the finished version at a later date. We'll include that in the next cycle.

Please remember, this is a voluntary project! If you have other stuff to do in life, please give that a higher priority.

Incase you can't make the deadline, or you have to abandon the work, or you need more time, please feel free to contact the core team for details. We'll figure this out.

Is the deadlines same for all kinds of submissions?

No, not at all. For some simple topics, the deadlines will be shorter - but doable. For some complex / hard topics, there will be a lot of provided.

I'm good with Honkai Impact 3rd more than Genshin. Can I contribute?

Yes ofcourse! As of 29th January 2023, a separate website exclusively for Honkai Impact 3rd is in pipeline. Stay tuned for a few weeks.
